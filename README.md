# Kampüsünden

**2025-2026 Spring CS310 Mobile Application Development Term Project**

## 📱 About The Project
A campus-exclusive, peer-to-peer marketplace developed with Flutter, allowing university students to safely buy, sell, or trade second-hand items (such as dorm appliances and books) through a privacy-focused, anonymous messaging system.

## 🎯 The Problem & Our Solution
University students frequently need to buy or sell short-term essentials (like mini-fridges or water heaters) and seasonal academic materials (like past exams or textbooks). Existing second-hand platforms require shipping, lack a trusted campus environment, and force students to interact with strangers. 

"Kampüsümden" solves this by providing a localized, commission-free ecosystem where transactions happen face-to-face on campus among verified students.
